Upload, sell and use Presentations with your students

Our platform allows you to upload, sell and use presentations for any subject with your students. Many research by American universities has shown that structured presentations with short explanations and well-chosen images increase learning productivity compared to regular textbooks. If you will use great presentations for your student's classwork and homework you will increase their knowledge about topics they learn.

The different with textbooks

The same information can be shown in two ways, with several pages of text, or only with the most important phrases, accompanied by a picture explaining 80% of the information.
People love to see pictures more than text, so children will be more interested to learn new information with presentations.

How to upload and sell your presentations

All you need is your presentation ready to upload in PDF and 5 minutes for settings. You can choose the price as you want or choose monetization by uses.
