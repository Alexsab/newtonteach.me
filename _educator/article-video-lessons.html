Video lessons

Create, sell and use Video Lessons with your students

New information is better perceived by children through videos with animation, graphic examples than through the usual textbooks with dry text and static pictures.

You can upload your video tutorials to the platform or use ready-made videos of other authors on the marketplace and apply them for homework or classwork of your class.

Some videos contain quizzes created by the author for the topic of a video lesson with several levels of difficulty, which allow you to immediately test and consolidate new knowledge by your students.

Upload your Video Lessons

Upload your lectures, lessons, or professional educational videos for any subject. You can earn money by publishing your video lessons on the marketplace, or you can use them only for your students and classwork.

Add video lesson to Homework and Classwork

Add yours or other video authors to your lessons. To do this, just select the required video in the list of your library.

You will know if your student watched only half of the video

If you have added any video on the platform to classwork or homework, then you will see the statistics of your students containing information about who watched the lesson, how long, and when.

Class report from homework or classwork

Upload video lesson is easy

All you need is your video ready to upload and 5 minutes for settings. You can choose the price as you want or choose monetization by watches. Then add a description, grades, subject, and standards. If you have made quizzes for the video, you can link them in settings anytime.

Video lessons are a great tool to increase students' interest in learning. If you create your own video lessons, you are making a huge contribution to education around the world by allowing other teachers and students to use your lessons.
