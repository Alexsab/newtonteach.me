Create, sell and use Quizzes with your students

Quizzes and Tests are used to check and consolidate students' knowledge to determine the knowledge level and select the optimal education program individually. You can create quizzes on the platform and immediately use them with your students, or send your quizzes to the marketplace to allow other teachers to use them for free, by subscription, or by purchasing them, which will get you additional passive income.

Using quizzes for your whole class will give you a detailed report for each student. You can also create or purchase quizzes of three levels of difficulty to share between your students, depending on their intellectual capabilities.
