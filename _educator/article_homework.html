Homework & Classwork

Assign Homeworks and Classworks to your students online
with all tasks and lessons included

You can create classwork and homework for your students in minutes and assign it asap or set a time and date. Add videos, quizzes, presentations, and files to your lessons, or even ask a student to upload files that you can check and grade by yourself on the platform. You can assign your lessons to the entire class or to individual students to personalize the education. Assign quizzes according to the level of difficulty among your students to match the assignment with knowledge and preparation.
