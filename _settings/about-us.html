About Newton Teach Me

Building the
future of education

Newton Teach Me is a new learning platform with a curriculum marketplace built for teachers who want to create and improve curriculum resources and use it to teach millions of students better.
Newton Teach Me is a place for educators and their students where they can receive and test their knowledge using the experience of thousands of educators - authors.
Newton Teach Me was founded in 2020 by a small team with the main goal: to improve the education system in the world and make the work of all teachers easier.
